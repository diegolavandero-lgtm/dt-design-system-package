Voice & content #voice

Operational, direct, bilingual (ES/EN), product-first. For dispatchers, fleet managers, and drivers — not consumers.

  • Imperative & concrete. Actions are verbs: "Crear orden", "Bloquear usuario", "Exportar a PlannerPro".
  • Second-person (tú/you). "Tienes cambios sin guardar. Si cierras este modal, los cambios se perderán."
  • Sentence case for all UI copy. UPPERCASE only for DS-internal section titles.
  • No emoji, no exclamations. Status is factual: "Activo", "Bloqueado", "Recogido".
  • Errors describe the condition, not the blame. "No pudimos geolocalizar esta dirección."
  • Numbers are first-class. "1–25 de 340", "10-10-25 10:35 hrs", "200/200 (100%)".
✓ Do
"Tienes cambios sin guardar. Si cierras este modal, los cambios se perderán."
✗ Don't
"¡Atención! Tu información se perderá!! Por favor guarda antes de salir 😊"

Colors #colors

One dark surface (Indigo, topbar only). Two surfaces stacked under it (N2 page · white card). Cobalt primary. Four semantic intents. Color is never decoration — every hue carries a role.
